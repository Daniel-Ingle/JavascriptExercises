Bouncy Ball

Stopped

Make a 'ball' (okay, a square... although bonus points for making it a ball) 'bounce' around a bounded. When it hits a wall, it 'bounces' and goes in the other direction. Start the ball moving by hitting the 'Enter' key, and stop it by pressing the 'Enter' key again.

Hints:

  • We can re-use a lot of the stuff from the snake game: wall collision-detection, showing the ball (like the 'apple' or the 'snake').
  • When you hit a wall, it should 'bounce.' If it is going up and to the right, and it hits the top wall, it should continue to move right, but it should switch to moving down.

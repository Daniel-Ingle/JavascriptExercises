Result

Given a Count, show that many numbers in the Fibonacci sequence.

What is the Fibonacci sequence? In mathematics, the Fibonacci numbers form a sequence, the Fibonacci sequence, in which each number is the sum of the two preceding ones. The sequence starts from 0 and 1. Thus, the next number in the sequence 0 + 1 = 1, forming 0,1,1. And the next number 1 + 1 = 2, forming 0,1,1,2. Next, 1 + 2 = 3, forming 0,1,1,2,3.

If given the Count 8, the sequence would thus be: [0,1,1,2,3,5,8,13].

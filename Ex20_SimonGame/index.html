Simon Game


Play the game of Simon.

Simon is a game where the computer selects one of four 'panels' for you to press. When you press the correct panel, Simon repeats the sequence and adds an additional, random panel to the sequence, making the number of panels you have to remember to press longer and longer.

Most of the game has been built: how to flash panels, etc. But what YOU need to do is figure out the logic behind processing the player's clicks: how to see if they enter the right sequence as well as determining when to add a new panel to the sequence or end the game.
